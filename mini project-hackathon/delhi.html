About Delhi Speedway

Delhi Speedway is one of the premier karting venues in the country, known for its dynamic track design and exceptional racing experience. Ideal for both amateurs and seasoned racers, this speedway offers a thrilling environment for karting enthusiasts.

Track Layout

Our track is designed to provide an exhilarating racing experience, featuring sharp turns, long straights, and elevation changes that challenge every driver's skill. The layout spans over 1.2 kilometers, ensuring an action-packed race every time.

Facilities

The Delhi Speedway offers a range of facilities to enhance your racing experience, including:

  • High-performance karts with safety equipment
  • Food and beverage stalls to recharge after racing
  • Viewing areas for spectators to cheer on their favorites
  • Special events and training sessions available

Location

Conveniently located in Delhi, the Speedway is easily accessible for all racing fans. Join us for a day of fun and excitement on the track!
