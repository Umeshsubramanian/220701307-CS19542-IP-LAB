About Mumbai Racing Association Track

The Mumbai Racing Association Track is a premier racing venue that offers thrilling karting experiences for enthusiasts of all skill levels. With a variety of track layouts and modern facilities, it is a favorite among racers.

Track Layout

Our track features a dynamic layout that spans over 1.2 kilometers, designed to provide both challenge and excitement. From hairpin turns to long straights, it caters to all racing styles.

Facilities

The Mumbai Racing Association Track provides top-notch amenities, including:

  • High-performance karts and safety gear
  • Refreshment area with a variety of snacks and beverages
  • Viewing galleries for friends and family to watch the races
  • Corporate events and training programs available

Location

Conveniently located in the heart of Mumbai, the Mumbai Racing Association Track is easily accessible for all racing enthusiasts. Come and experience the thrill!
