About Hyderabad International Circuit

The Hyderabad International Circuit is a premier racing venue known for its challenging layout and vibrant motorsports culture. With state-of-the-art facilities, it provides a thrilling experience for both racers and spectators.

Track Layout

The circuit features a dynamic layout with a length of approximately 2.5 kilometers, incorporating various elevation changes and a mix of fast straights and technical corners.

Facilities

Hyderabad International Circuit offers a range of modern amenities, including:

  • High-performance karts and safety equipment
  • Hospitality suites with viewing balconies
  • Food and beverage outlets for spectators
  • Corporate events and karting lessons available

Location

Conveniently located just outside the city, the Hyderabad International Circuit is easily accessible and ready to host a variety of racing events, making it a must-visit for motorsport enthusiasts.
