About Bangalore Kart Arena

The Bangalore Kart Arena is one of the premier karting venues in India, known for its thrilling track layout and state-of-the-art facilities. Whether you are a seasoned racer or a newcomer to the sport, our arena offers an exhilarating experience for everyone.

Track Layout

Our track spans over 1 kilometer, featuring a mix of tight corners and long straights designed to challenge drivers and maximize the fun. With a smooth surface and safety barriers, it's the perfect place to race.

Facilities

The Bangalore Kart Arena boasts modern amenities including:

  • Professional karts and safety gear
  • Refreshment area with snacks and drinks
  • Spectator viewing areas for friends and family
  • Training sessions and corporate events available

Location

The Bangalore Kart Arena is conveniently located in the heart of the city, making it accessible for all racing enthusiasts. Join us for an unforgettable experience!
