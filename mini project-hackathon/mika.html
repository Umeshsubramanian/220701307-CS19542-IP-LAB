Madras International Karting Arena

Experience the thrill of karting on one of India's top circuits!

Track Layout

The Madras International Karting Arena is a state-of-the-art karting facility, located just outside Chennai. The track offers a perfect blend of high-speed straights, tight corners, and technical sections. Designed for all levels of drivers, the track promises excitement and challenges, whether you're a beginner or an experienced racer.

Track Features

  • Track Length: 1.2 km
  • Track Width: 8 meters
  • 16 high-speed, challenging corners
  • Professional-grade safety barriers and lighting
  • Fast straight sections for high-speed thrills
  • State-of-the-art timing systems
  • Ideal for both amateurs and professionals
